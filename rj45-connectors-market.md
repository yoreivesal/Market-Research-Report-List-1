<p><p>RJ45 connectors are essential for Ethernet networking, available in shielded and unshielded types. Shielded RJ45 connectors feature additional protective materials to reduce electromagnetic interference (EMI), making them ideal for environments with high levels of interference. Conversely, unshielded RJ45 connectors are simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for standard networking applications with minimal EMI. The market demand varies, with shielded connectors gaining traction in industrial and commercial settings, while unshielded connectors dominate residential and basic office applications.</p></p>
