<p><p>Dental micro-sandblasters are categorized into three main types based on their tank configuration. The 2-tank system features separate tanks for the abrasive material and air, allowing for more precise control and reduced cross-contamination. The 1-tank system combines both functions into a single unit, which can be more compact and cost-effective, but may limit versatility. Other types may include specialized systems with unique features or modifications suited for specific dental applications, enhancing efficiency and user preference.</p></p>
