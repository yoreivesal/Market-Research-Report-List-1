<p><p>The in-line valves market includes three main types: manual valves, pneumatic valves, and electromagnetic valves. Manual valves are operated by hand, providing control over flow and pressure within a system. Pneumatic valves utilize compressed air to automate the flow control, enhancing system efficiency and responsiveness. Each type serves specific applications, contributing to improved operational performance in various industrial settings.</p></p>
