<p><p>The Orally Dissolving Strip market comprises primarily two types: Orodispersible Films and Oromucosal Films. Orodispersible Films dissolve upon contact with saliva, delivering quick drug absorption and convenience for patients requiring fast action, such as those with nausea or pain. Oromucosal Films, on the other hand, are designed for mucosal administration, providing localized therapy or systemic effects while enhancing bioavailability. Both types cater to diverse therapeutic areas, emphasizing ease of use and improved patient compliance in drug delivery systems.</p></p>
