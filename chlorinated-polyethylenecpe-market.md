<p><p>Chlorinated Polyethylene (CPE) is a versatile material categorized into two primary market types: thermoplastic resin type and elastomer rubber type. The thermoplastic resin type offers excellent chemical resistance and thermal stability, making it suitable for applications in automotive and construction industries. In contrast, the elastomer rubber type exhibits high flexibility, toughness, and impact resistance, making it ideal for seals, gaskets, and various industrial products. Both types leverage CPE's adaptability, enhancing performance across numerous sectors.</p></p>
