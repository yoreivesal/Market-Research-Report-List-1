<p><p>The civil aircraft weighing system market comprises two main types: platform and floor-standing systems. Platform systems utilize a flat surface where aircraft are positioned for accurate measurement, ideal for smaller or regional aircraft. In contrast, floor-standing systems incorporate vertical structures that allow for larger aircraft to be weighed without necessitating additional lifting equipment. Both types ensure precise weight measurements essential for safety, compliance, and performance optimization within the aviation industry.</p></p>
