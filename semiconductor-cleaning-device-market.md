In contrast, tank cleaning equipment involves larger tanks used for cleaning multiple semiconductor wafers simultaneously, often employing chemical baths to remove contaminants. Both types cater to the rigorous cleanliness standards required in semiconductor manufacturing, enhancing product yield and reliability.</p></p>
