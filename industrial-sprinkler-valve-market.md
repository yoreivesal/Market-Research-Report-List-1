<p><p>The industrial sprinkler valve market features two primary types: linear spray valves and radial spray valves. Linear spray valves deliver a focused stream of water in a straight line, effective for protecting long, narrow areas. In contrast, radial spray valves emit water in a circular pattern, providing widespread coverage for larger spaces. Both valve types are crucial for fire suppression systems, ensuring optimal safety and efficiency in industrial settings by addressing varying spatial requirements and fire risks.</p></p>
