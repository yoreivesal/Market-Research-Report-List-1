<p><p>The dental barrier membrane bone graft market is segmented into absorbable and non-absorbable membranes. Absorbable membranes, made from materials that naturally break down in the body, promote healing without the need for removal, making them convenient for patients. Non-absorbable membranes, typically crafted from synthetic materials, provide longer-lasting structural support but require a secondary procedure for removal. Both types play crucial roles in guided bone regeneration and dental implant procedures, enhancing bone healing and architectural stability.</p></p>
